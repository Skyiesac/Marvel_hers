ABOUT

Marvel Comics is a popular American comic book publisher that was founded in 1939. The company is known for its creation of iconic superheroes such as Spider-Man, Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, and the Hulk. Marvel Comics has published over 10,000 comic book titles and has expanded into other media such as movies, television, video games, and toys.
Marvel Studios is a film production company that was founded in 1996. The studio is responsible for producing the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), a series of interconnected films that are based on Marvel Comics characters. The MCU has been a huge success, and has grossed over $25 billion worldwide.
The Marvel Cinematic Universe began in 2008 with the release of Iron Man. Since then, the MCU has released 24 films.
The success of the Marvel Cinematic Universe has helped to make Marvel Comics one of the most popular brands in the world. Marvel Studios has also helped to popularize the superhero genre, and has inspired other studios to produce their own superhero films.
The future of Marvel and Marvel Studios looks bright. The company has a number of upcoming projects in development, including Avengers: Infinity War, Black Panther, and Captain Marvel. Marvel is also expanding into new areas, such as
streaming television and theme parks.
